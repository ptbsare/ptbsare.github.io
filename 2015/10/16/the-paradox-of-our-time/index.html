By ptbsare

10月 16 2015 更新日期:10月 19 2015

文章目录

We talk too much, love too seldom, and hate too often. We’ve learned how to make a living, but not a life; we’ve added years to life, not life to years.”
　　　　　　-George Carlin 1937-2008
